If you have been logging plays in BG Stats, you can bring your entire history into WR-Games without re-entering anything. The import wizard walks you through matching players, locations, and conflicts before anything is committed.

## What You'll Need

Export a backup file from the BG Stats app on your phone. BG Stats calls this a "backup" or "export" in its Settings screen — it produces a `.json` file on your device.

## How to Import

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Import Wizard">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations → BG Stats Import**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Your Backup File">
    In the BG Stats app, open **Settings** and export a backup. Save the `.json` file somewhere accessible on your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the File">
    On WR-Games, upload the `.json` backup file. WR-Games reads it and queues it for processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work Through the Wizard">
    The wizard walks you through four screens in order:

    - **Players** — Match player names from your BG Stats data to your WR-Games friends. Names that don't match become guest entries.
    - **Locations** — Match your BG Stats location names to WR-Games venues, or mark them as custom locations.
    - **Conflicts** — Review any plays that overlap with existing entries and decide whether to merge or skip them.
    - **Review** — See a full summary of everything that will be imported before you commit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Commit the Import">
    Click **Import** on the Review screen. Your plays are added to your personal play history immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Re-importing the same BG Stats backup file is safe. WR-Games detects plays it has already seen and skips them — you will not end up with duplicates if you run the import more than once.
</Note>
