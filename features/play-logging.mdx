WR-Games gives you a native play log that works whether you are recording a game you just finished at an event or catching up on a session from last weekend. Your play history lives on your profile, and when you tag other players in a play, everyone gets a shared social record of the session — no third-party app required.

## Logging a Play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Play Log">
    Go to your profile or the main hub and select **Plays**, then click **Log a Play**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Game">
    Search for the game title. WR-Games looks up the game in its verified catalog so all your plays are linked to the correct entry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the Date">
    Choose the date the session took place. You can log plays retroactively, so there is no need to record in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Players, Scores, and Expansions">
    Search for WR-Games friends to tag them as co-players, or type in a guest name for anyone not on the platform. Optionally record each player's score and note which expansions were in use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Play">
    Click **Save**. The play is added to your personal play history immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Personal History vs. Shared Plays

Every play you log lands in your **personal play history** — a record that belongs to you and follows your profile. When you tag other WR-Games users as co-players, the play also becomes a **shared social record** visible to all tagged participants.

Being tagged in someone else's play does **not** automatically add it to your personal history. You will see a notification, and you can choose to **Copy to My History** if you want it counted in your stats. This keeps your personal record clean and under your control.

<Note>
  Only you can add plays to your personal history. A tag from another player is an invitation, not an automatic entry.
</Note>

## Play Privacy

Your play history is visible on your public profile by default. If you prefer to keep it private, go to **Settings → Privacy** and turn off **Show play history on profile**. Your plays remain intact — they are simply hidden from anyone viewing your profile.
