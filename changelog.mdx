Here's what's new on WR-Games — newest first.

<Update label="June 2026" tags={["Integrations","Calendar","Platform"]}>
  ## Connect your Meetup group to WR-Games

  Already running your group on Meetup? You can now connect it to WR-Games and bring your events over — sync them in to sit side by side with your WR-Games events so members can RSVP without leaving, or simply link out to Meetup. A quick **Refresh** pulls the latest whenever you cancel or update something on Meetup. [See how the Meetup integration works](https://help.wr-games.com/groups/meetup-integration).

  ## Your BoardGameGeek collection, fully under your control

  Syncing your BGG collection is faster and far more flexible. Your games show up almost immediately while the details fill in behind the scenes, you choose which collections to pull when you first connect, and you can reset or wipe your synced collection whenever you want. [Learn how BGG sync and your Collection work](https://help.wr-games.com/features/bgg-collection).

  ## Add custom entries to your group calendar

  Mark up your group's calendar with custom entries — birthdays, reminders, social nights, whatever your group needs. They save automatically, show up for everyone in the group, and flow into any subscribed calendar feed. [See group calendar custom entries](https://help.wr-games.com/groups/group-calendar-custom-entries).

  ## A new support center at help.wr-games.com

  We launched a support center — clear, searchable guides for groups, events, RSVPs, integrations, and more, so you can get an answer fast instead of guessing. This changelog lives here too. [Visit the support center](https://help.wr-games.com).
</Update>
