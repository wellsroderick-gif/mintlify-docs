If your game collection already lives on BGG, there is no need to rebuild it from scratch on WR-Games. Connecting your BGG account pulls in your collections automatically, organises them in your WR-Games collection, and unlocks notifications that fire whenever a game you want to play shows up in an upcoming event near you.

<Note>
  WR-Games only reads your BGG collection data. It never writes back to BGG or modifies your BGG profile, ratings, or collections in any way.
</Note>

## How to Connect Your BGG Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your BGG Username">
    Type your username into the **BGG Username** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Which Collections to Import">
    WR-Games shows a list of your BGG collection categories. **Owned** and **Want to Play** are checked by default — these are the most useful for finding and attending local events. Toggle any of the other collections on or off to match what you care about.

    <Note>
      WR-Games plans to use your collection data to power more features over time — for example, games in your **Want to Buy** or **Preordered** collections may be used to surface alerts and recommendations in the future.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Click **Start sync**. WR-Games begins fetching your collection from BGG.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the Sync to Complete">
    The sync typically finishes in under a minute. You will see a confirmation once your games are ready in your collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use the **Select All** and **Clear All** buttons above the category list to configure your collections quickly without toggling each one individually.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Want to Play** games import into their own folder. When you open your Collection page, it defaults to showing your **Owned** games — switch to the Want to Play tab to see that collection.
</Note>

## What Gets Synced

WR-Games reads up to 7 BGG collection categories. You choose which ones to include:

| Collection | What it includes |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Want to Play** | Games on your BGG Want to Play list |
| **Owned** | Games you have marked as owned |
| **Wishlist** | Games you want to acquire |
| **For Trade** | Games you are willing to trade away |
| **Want to Buy** | Games you are actively looking to purchase |
| **Preordered** | Games you have on preorder |
| **Previously Owned** | Games you owned in the past |

<Note>
  Your **Want to Play** list and your **WR-Games Favorites** are the two that trigger event notifications — save a game to either and you'll hear about local events featuring it near you.
</Note>

## After Syncing

Once the sync completes, your games appear in your **Collection** tab, organised by the collection categories you enabled. From there you can browse by collection, search within your collection, and view each game's detail page.

## Re-Syncing

Your BGG collection does not update automatically on a set schedule. After your first sync, the Integrations card switches to a **Save & Re-Sync** view — return here any time to add or remove collections, or to pull in recent BGG changes like newly acquired games and updated collection assignments.

<Note>
  **Auto-sync is coming soon.** We're working on automatic BGG collection updates so you don't have to re-sync manually.
</Note>

## Resetting or Wiping Your Collection

The **Danger Zone** in **Settings → Integrations** gives you two options for clearing your synced data. Neither action affects your BGG account.

<Warning>
  **Reset BGG sync** removes all BGG-synced games from your WR-Games collection and clears the last-synced time. It is recoverable — go back to Integrations and re-sync anytime to pull your games back in.
</Warning>

<Danger>
  **Wipe collection** permanently removes your entire WR-Games collection and disconnects BGG. This cannot be undone. To start over, reconnect your BGG account and re-sync from scratch.
</Danger>
