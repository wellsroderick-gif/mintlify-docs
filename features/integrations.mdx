WR-Games connects with the platforms you already use to track games and plays. Each integration is optional and independent — set up the ones that fit how you game. Everything lives in **Settings → Integrations**.

  <Card title="BGG Sync" icon="database" href="/features/bgg-sync">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Board Game Arena" icon="link" href="/features/bga">
    Add your BGA username so other WR-Games players know where to find you for online play.
  </Card>

  <Card title="BG Stats Import" icon="download" href="/features/bg-stats">
    Bring your full play history from BG Stats into WR-Games without re-entering anything manually.
