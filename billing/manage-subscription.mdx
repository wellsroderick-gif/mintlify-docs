You can review and change your WR-Games subscription at any time without contacting support. Whether you want to unlock more features, scale back, or simply check a past invoice, everything you need is a few clicks away in your account settings. This page walks you through each common billing task.

## Finding Your Billing Settings

Go to **Settings → Billing** (also accessible via **Account → Subscription** in the top-right menu). From there you can see your current plan, next renewal date, credit balance, and billing history.

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Billing">
    Select your current plan tile or click the **Upgrade** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan and billing cycle">
    Pick Standard or Pro, then choose **monthly** or **annual** billing. Annual billing saves you 20% — Standard is $8/month ($96/year) and Pro is $16/month ($192/year).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You'll be taken to a secure checkout page to confirm payment. Your upgrade takes effect **immediately** once the payment is processed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrading Your Plan

To move to a lower tier, go to **Settings → Billing** and select the plan you'd like to switch to. Your current plan remains active until the end of your billing period — you won't lose access to your current features mid-cycle. At renewal, your account moves to the new plan.

<Warning>
  **Check your usage before you downgrade.** If your current setup exceeds the limits of the lower plan, some features will become restricted at the end of your billing period. For example, the Free plan allows only **1 group** — if you have 3 groups on Standard and downgrade to Free, you'll need to consolidate to 1 group before the transition to avoid losing access to the additional groups' content and settings. Review your groups, roles, and any active features ahead of time.
</Warning>

## Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel at any time from **Settings → Billing** by selecting **Cancel Plan**. Cancellation does not cut off access immediately — your paid plan stays active through the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the **Free plan** automatically. You won't be charged again unless you re-subscribe.

## Annual vs. Monthly Billing

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (billed yearly) | You save |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard | $10 / month | $8 / month ($96/year) | 20% |
| Pro | $20 / month | $16 / month ($192/year) | 20% |

You can switch between monthly and annual billing from **Settings → Billing**. Switching to annual applies at your next renewal date; switching from annual to monthly takes effect when your current annual term ends.

<Tip>
  If you're sure you'll use WR-Games year-round, annual billing is the easiest way to save — two months free compared to paying month to month.
</Tip>

## Viewing Invoices and Receipts

All invoices and receipts are available through the **Stripe Customer Portal**, linked from **Settings → Billing → Billing History**. From the portal you can:

- Download PDF invoices for any past payment
- View upcoming charge dates and amounts
- Update your payment method or billing address

<Note>
  WR-Games billing is handled securely by **Stripe**, a globally trusted payment processor. WR-Games never stores your full card details — all payment information is managed directly by Stripe.
</Note>
