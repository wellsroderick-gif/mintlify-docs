If you play on Board Game Arena, you can add your BGA username to your WR-Games profile. Once set, it appears on your public profile so other players know where to find you for online games.

## How to Add Your BGA Username

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your BGA Username">
    Find the **Board Game Arena** section and enter your username.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. Your BGA username now appears on your public WR-Games profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This is display-only — WR-Games does not import your BGA game history, play logs, or ratings.
</Note>
