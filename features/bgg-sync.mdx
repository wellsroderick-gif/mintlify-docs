If your game collection already lives on BoardGameGeek (BGG), there is no need to rebuild it from scratch on WR-Games. Connecting your BGG account pulls in your shelves automatically, organises them in your WR-Games collection, and unlocks notifications that fire whenever a game you own — or want to play — shows up in an upcoming event near you.

## How to Connect Your BGG Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your BGG Username">
    Type your BGG username into the **BGG Username** field. You do not need your BGG password — WR-Games only reads your public collection data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Which Collections to Import">
    WR-Games shows a list of your BGG shelf categories. **Owned** and **Want to Play** are checked by default — these are the most useful shelves for finding and attending local events. Toggle any of the other six shelves on or off to match what you care about.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Click **Start sync**. WR-Games begins fetching your collection from BGG.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the Sync to Complete">
    The sync typically finishes in under a minute. You will see a confirmation once your games are ready in your collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use the **Select All** and **Clear All** buttons above the category list to configure your shelves quickly without toggling each one individually.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Want to Play** games import into their own folder. When you open your Collection page, it defaults to showing your **Owned** games — switch to the Want to Play tab to see that shelf.
</Note>

## What Gets Synced

WR-Games reads up to 8 BGG shelf categories. You choose which ones to include:

<Accordion title="Which BGG shelves can sync?">
  - **Owned** — Games you have marked as owned on BGG
  - **Want to Play** — Games on your Want to Play list
  - **Wishlist** — Games you want to acquire
  - **For Trade** — Games you are willing to trade away
  - **Want to Buy** — Games you are actively looking to purchase
  - **Preordered** — Games you have on preorder
  - **Previously Owned** — Games you owned in the past
  - **Favorites** — Games you have rated 8 or higher on BGG
</Accordion>

## After Syncing

Once the sync completes, your games appear in your **Collection** tab, organised by the shelf categories you enabled. From there you can browse by shelf, search within your collection, and view each game's detail page.

WR-Games also uses your synced collection to send you event notifications. When an upcoming event near you lists a game from your Owned or Want to Play shelf, you will receive a notification so you never miss a chance to play something you care about.

## Re-Syncing

Your BGG collection does not update automatically on a set schedule. After your first sync, the Integrations card switches to a **Save & Re-Sync** view — return here any time to add or remove shelves, or to pull in recent BGG changes like newly acquired games and updated shelf assignments.

## Adding Your Board Game Arena Username

In the same **Settings → Integrations** section, you can enter your **Board Game Arena (BGA) username**. This links your BGA profile to your WR-Games identity for display purposes, letting other players find and recognise you across platforms.

<Note>
  WR-Games only reads your BGG collection data. It never writes back to BGG or modifies your BGG profile, ratings, or shelves in any way.
</Note>
